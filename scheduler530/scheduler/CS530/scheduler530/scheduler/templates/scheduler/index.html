ABOUT US

~ find out about how it all started ~

Once upon a time in the distant Fall 2015 semester, Megan, Tina, Bryan, Jared, and Jacob registered for 437. Upon enrolling in the course, they set out on a dangerous journey to design their own product as a team of software engineers and product managers. This is how 50 Shades of Webstac was born. Through tiring battles with pivots from their MVP's, Django/Python learning curves, and end user opinions, the team developed a four year schedule creater for several majors, hopefully passing the test to become the next 437 kings and queens.
